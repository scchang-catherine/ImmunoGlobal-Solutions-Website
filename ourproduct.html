Product Mechanism

Skeletal muscle proteins (SMP) in animals function together with growth factors to modulate the differentiation and growth of muscles. The expression of SMP directly correlates with the age; younger animals express lower amounts of SMP while more mature animals express larger amounts of SMP, which inhibits muscle growth. To prevent the diminishing of muscle growth in older animals, we utilize MyoRock, a type of coated protein that can safely bypass stomach acid, to compete with the regular SMPs and thus perpetuate the growth of muscle cells.
